Frequently Asked Questions

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. All payments are processed through Stripe, a certified PCI Service Provider Level 1. We do not store any of your credit card information on our servers.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You will retain your premium benefits until the end of your current billing period.

What happens after I make the payment?

Your account will be upgraded instantly. You will have immediate access to all the premium features included in your chosen plan. A confirmation email and receipt will be sent to your registered email address.
